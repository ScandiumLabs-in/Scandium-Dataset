# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
Scandium Dataset project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable
behavior and are expected to take appropriate corrective action in response
to any violation.
